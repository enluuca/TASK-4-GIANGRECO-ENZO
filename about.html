General Infomation

The Migthy Ducks Soccer League was establised in 1996 to provide athletes residing Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play hockey. To be a member of MDHL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside a Chicago northside neighborhoods. MDHL ran by o small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members
